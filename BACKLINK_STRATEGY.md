## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips

1. **Quality > Quantity**
   - 1 link from Forbes > 100 links from spam sites
   
2. **Relevance Matters**
   - Link from HVAC blog > Link from cooking blog
   
3. **Anchor Text Variety**
   - Use: "Autoquill AI", "this AI tool", "autoquillai.com", "here"
   - Don't overuse: "best AI receptionist" (looks spammy)
   
4. **Build Relationships First**
   - Don't immediately ask for links
   - Provide value first
   - Links come naturally
   
5. **Be Newsworthy**
   - Hit a milestone? Press release it
   - Unique data? Share it everywhere
   - Controversy? (Carefully) use it
